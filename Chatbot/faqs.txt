Q: What are your shipping options?
A: We offer standard and express shipping. Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days, and express takes 2-3 business days.

Q: How do I return a product?
A: You can return most products within 30 days of purchase. Visit our 'Returns' page on the website to start the process.

Q: How do I track my order?
A: You can track your order status by logging into your account and clicking on the 'My Orders' section.

Q: How do I contact customer service?
A: You can reach our customer service team by calling 1-800-555-0199 or by emailing support@marketplace.com.
